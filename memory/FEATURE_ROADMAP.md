---

## Feature Suggestions

### **Tier 1: Core Features** (High Impact)

1. **🏆 Early Discovery Bonus** ✅ IMPLEMENTED v1
   - Users who invest in a video *before* it trends get bonus multiplier
   - Rewards genuine curation, not bandwagon following

2. **💬 Commenter Rewards System**
   - Top comments earn micro-shares of the video
   - Community upvotes determine "valuable" comments

3. **🔗 Curator Earnings**
   - Share a video → someone invests through your link → you earn %
   - "Referral shares" - permanent small cut of future trades

4. **📊 Transparent Revenue Dashboard** ✅ IMPLEMENTED v1
   - Show exactly where revenue goes: Creator %, Shareholders %, Platform %

5. **💰 Dividend Payouts**
   - Video ad revenue distributed to shareholders proportionally
   - Weekly/monthly payouts based on views generated

### **Tier 2: Democratization Features** (Medium Impact)

6. **🗳️ Shareholder Governance**
   - Major shareholders vote on creator decisions
   - Platform-wide votes on feature changes

7. **⭐ Quality Score (Not Virality)**
   - Algorithm that rewards engagement quality over quantity
   - Completion rate, comment depth, re-watches weighted higher

8. **🎯 Fair Launch System**
   - All new videos start at same base price
   - Level playing field for emerging creators

### **Tier 3: Advanced Features** (Future Vision)

9. **🔐 Content Ownership Verification**
   - Proof of creation timestamp
   - Portable ownership records

10. **📈 Creator IPO Events**
    - New creators "go public" with limited initial shares
    - Early supporters get founder pricing
